Solar Access Site Plan

Site Name: Solar Acess

Reason:

The site name reflect the mission of making solar energy more accessible and affordable to middle-class families. The name is short, memorable, and clearly communicates the site's purpose.

Site Purpose:

The purpose of the website is to educate and guide middle-income households on how they can adopt solar energy through affordable community leveraging, bulk installation options, and financing strategies. It aims to raise awareness, provide resources, and connect interested families with vetted service providers.

Scenarios:

  • Is there a way can I install solar panels in my home without paying everything upfront?
  • What are the financial and environmental benefits of switching to solar energy?

Color Scheme:

The selected color scheme supports the solar and energy theme with professional contrast:

  • #043927 (Dark Green) - used for headers, navigation bar, and emphasis
  • #f4c430 (Solar Yellow) - used for call-to-action buttons, highlights, and icons

Typography:

  • Playfair Display - used for headers to give a strong, confident feel
  • Roboto - used for body text to ensure readability and modern presentation
